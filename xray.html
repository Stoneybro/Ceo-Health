X-ray

At UVA Radiology and Medical Imaging, we offer X-ray tests that produce images of the structures inside your body, especially bones. Our quick and painless tests will help diagnose bone fractures, arthritis, osteoporosis, infections, breast cancer, digestive tract problems, swallowed items, and more. During the test, you will lie, sit, or stand while the X-ray machine takes images. Our experienced technicians may ask you to move into several positions to capture the best images. The entire process takes only 10-15 minutes, and the results will be available to your doctor promptly. It is important to note that X-rays use ionizing radiation, which can be harmful in large doses. However, the amount of radiation used in our tests is very low, and we take every precaution to ensure your safety. Our team of experts uses the latest technology and techniques to provide accurate results while minimizing radiation exposure. At UVA Radiology and Medical Imaging, we are committed to providing the highest quality care for our patients. Our team is dedicated to making the process as comfortable and convenient as possible. Do not hesitate to seek the necessary clarifications. Call us today to schedule your X-ray test and take the first step towards better health.
