Muscle Recovery

OVERVIEW

An IV program for muscle recovery is a great way to combat inflammation and help your body heal quickly. This program combines IV vitamins and minerals with innovative therapies to reduce inflammation and encourage natural healing. The program is designed to be tailored to fit each patient's needs, making it an effective way of addressing the individual requirements of each person. The program works by delivering the correct combination of micronutrients directly into the bloodstream, which helps reduce inflammation and stimulate muscle healing. In addition, a specialized blend of vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, amino acids and other nutrients helps further reduce inflammation. With all these aspects combined, the program works to increase energy levels while reducing pain, fatigue and tension in areas that have been subject to physical stress or strain.

What's In The IV?

The program includes small and carefully monitored doses of Vitamin B classes 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6; Vitamin C; Magnesium; Carnitine; and Arginine. Each one of these vitamins and minerals plays an essential role in providing cells with the nourishment they need to rebuild and repair muscle tissue so together they work to reduce inflammation and give your body a much-needed boost after strenuous activity.

Who's It For?

Our IV program for muscle recovery is suitable for people of all ages who are looking to reduce inflammation and muscle soreness. Whether you are an athlete looking to enhance your performance or just want to recover quickly from your workout, this program is designed to help you achieve your goals.
