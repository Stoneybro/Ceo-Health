Anti-aging, Wrinkles and Acne

OVERVIEW

Our Skin Brightening IV Program is specially formulated to help you achieve brighter, firmer and more even skin tone. With the addition of signature peptides and amino acids, this program will restore optimal hydration while improving skin texture. By infusing pure vitamins and antioxidants directly into your bloodstream, our program helps reduce the appearance of wrinkles, brightens dull skin and repairs underlying damage to your skin’s collagen. The result is a glowing complexion that looks refreshed and revitalized with no downtime needed. Each IV session is carefully monitored by one of our experienced medical practitioners to ensure the highest quality results.

What's In The IV?

It includes Vitamin B2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 to increase skin hydration for a smoother appearance. Vitamin C and Magnesium are added to protect the skin from free radical damage that can cause wrinkles and uneven pigmentation. Carnitine works by stimulating the production of collagen, while Arginine helps break down carbohydrates and fats to help reduce puffiness in the face, eyes and neck.

Who's It For?

The Skin Brightening IV Program is designed for anyone looking to achieve a more even skin tone and brighter complexion. It is perfect for those who want to reduce dark spots, diminish redness, and lighten hyper-pigmentation while nourishing their skin from the inside out.

    Benefits

  • ● Once your course of treatments is complete, you will be able to enjoy clearer, brighter, younger-looking skin with a healthy and even skin tone.

  • ● During each infusion, vitamin C and other essential vitamins are delivered directly into the bloodstream, helping to reduce pigmentation and uneven tone.

  • ● The treatment also helps to improve hydration levels and reduce inflammation in the skin, giving you an instantly brightened complexion.

  • ● For best results, this treatment should be combined with a good skincare routine that includes sunscreen, moisturizer and antioxidant rich serums.
